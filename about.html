About She's Business

A love letter to businesswomen worldwide - Female student-led initiative and research

Our Mission

We empower and recognize aspiring and established business women worldwide. Through community, resources, and opportunities, we're building a network that supports and celebrates women in business at every stage of their journey.

"A love letter to businesswomen worldwide"

Founded in 2024, SheBusiness has grown from a passionate student-led initiative into a global community reaching thousands of women entrepreneurs and future leaders.
