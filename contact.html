Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a SheBusiness chapter at my school?

You can start a chapter by filling out our Chapter Application form on the Get Involved page. We'll guide you through the process and provide all the resources you need to get started, including marketing materials, event ideas, and ongoing support from our team.

How can I feature my business on your platform?

We'd love to feature your business! Use our Business Promotion Application on the Get Involved page. We spotlight female-owned businesses free of charge on our Instagram and website to help you reach our community of 2,000+ followers and gain valuable exposure.

Are there any costs to join SheBusiness programs?

All our programs and features are completely free! We believe in accessible entrepreneurship education and support. Whether you're starting a chapter, getting your business featured, or joining our community events, there are no costs involved.

How can I collaborate with SheBusiness?

We're always open to collaborations! Whether you're an organization, educator, or fellow entrepreneur, please reach out via our contact form or email us directly at talkshesbusiness@gmail.com. Let us know your ideas and how we can work together to empower more women in business.

What types of events does SheBusiness host?

We host various events including workshops on business innovation, networking sessions, panel discussions with successful entrepreneurs, and skill-building webinars. Follow our Instagram @shes.business for event announcements, or check our website for upcoming activities.
